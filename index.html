About Tawasul project

Charity project aims to provide educational and social assistance and participate in charitable campaigns aimed at helping people.

Our Services

Educational services

We provide a range of free educational courses in various fields such as psychological support, mathematics, physics and etc.

Feeding campaigns

We regularly carry out feeding campaigns for people in need. We distribute about 3,000 meals per campaign.

Street decorating

Every time we choose a street, we will decorate its walls with beautiful drawings that express hope and optimism.

social services

We go to the asylum centers to help those present and provide some tips as well as some activities to entertain the refugees.
